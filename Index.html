Privacy Policy for NEON NOCTIS

Effective Date: July 2026

1. Data Collection and Purpose

We value your privacy. In NEON NOCTIS, we do not collect personal information by default.

  • Unranked Mode: Playing in Unranked mode does not involve the collection or storage of any personal data.
  • Ranked Mode (Top 3 Leaderboard): If you reach the Top 3 in our global leaderboard or weekly leaderboard, you have the voluntary option to enter a nickname (alias). This nickname is stored on our secure database for the sole purpose of displaying your rank and name to other players on the in-game leaderboard.
  • 1v1 Multiplayer Mode (Anonymous Matchmaking): In our 1-on-1 multiplayer mode, players compete against each other anonymously. No real names, usernames, or personal profiles are shared with or visible to other players during gameplay. To facilitate and synchronize the connection between players, temporary session identifiers and network data (such as temporary IP addresses) are processed strictly for the duration of the match. This technical data is not stored permanently or used to track you.
  • Push Notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging / FCM): To keep you informed about leaderboard rank changes, we use Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM). For this service to function, a temporary, anonymous device identifier (FCM Token) is processed. This token is required solely to route the notification to your specific device. We do not use this identifier to track your behavior outside the game or to build advertisement profiles.

2. Your Rights and Control

You are in full control of your data:

  • Voluntary Participation: Entering a nickname is entirely optional and only available if you reach the Top 3.
  • Self-Service Deletion: You can remove your nickname and leaderboard entry at any time directly within the leaderboard menu by clicking the "DELETE" button next to your entry. This action permanently deletes your nickname and associated score from our database.
  • Opt-Out of Notifications: You can easily disable push notifications at any time in your Android device settings.
  • Contact & Support: If you have any further questions regarding your data or wish to request data deletion manually, you can contact us at schlachthaus.tv@web.de.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase (a service provided by Google Ireland Limited) to host the leaderboard database, handle matchmaking, and send push notifications. Your data is transferred and stored securely in accordance with standard industry practices. We do not share, sell, or use your data for advertising, marketing, or cross-app tracking purposes.
